What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn like humans.

Applications of AI

  • Machine Learning
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Computer Vision
  • Robotics
  • Speech Recognition

Benefits of AI

AI has the potential to revolutionize various industries, making processes more efficient, and solving complex problems.
